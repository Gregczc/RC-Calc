Flight Time Logbook

This tool uses a simple multiplication in order to determine the maximum range and flight time that would have been reachable if the battery was fully used. You will need to provide the flight time and energy usage from real flight in addition the max battery capacity.
This result it self is not the point. What's interesting is to log as many flight as possible for a given configuration. So whenever a change is made on a plane (propeller, motor, winglets etc.) you can compare the new performance to the old one in order to get an idea about the gain or loss in efficiency of the new setup.
